Problem Statement

The Challenge

The housing market is influenced by a complex web of variables — age of the property, renovation history, basement area, number of bedrooms and bathrooms, floor count, and many more. Making sense of this complexity requires powerful data visualization.

Our project addresses the challenge of extracting meaningful patterns from a large-scale housing dataset containing over 21,000 records. We aim to reveal how these features collectively influence sale prices, renovation trends, and market dynamics.
